The Flowerpot

An image/HTML/AJAX/iFrame overlay loader, dual-licensed under MIT and GPL (v2 or later). Easy, modern, pretty, and all that jazz. Get it at github.com/tofumatt/jquery.flowerpot/. Matt was responsible.
